About

Hi there! I’m Smriti Suresh, a 23 year old grad student currently in the second year of my Master’s degree in Computer Science at Boston University. I am passionate about Natural Language Processing and all things data. After obtaining my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science & Engineering from Mahindra Ecole Centrale (now Mahindra University) in Hyderabad, India, I knew I wanted to delve into the intricacies of how natural language and computing combine to produce mind-boggling results (GenAI is insane right now :o). I was deemed the Valedictorian of my Undergraduate Batch (2018-2022), obtaining the highest GPA among all students in the graduating batch as well as the CSE department. As a congratulatory honor, I was presented with 2 Gold Medals from Mr. Anand Mahindra and Mr. K. T. Rama Rao at the Annual Convocation Ceremony of July 2022.

As a highlight of 2023 and one of my proudest moments, I got to present my first research paper at an international conference! I’ve always been interested in research, undertaking several projects right from my undergrad days. One of those projects, excitingly enough, was recently accepted and published into one of the most renowned conferences - the ACM Web Conference 2023 as a part of the WWW’23 SocialNLP Workshop track. More about that in the Projects section.

Right now, I am focusing on my Graduate level courses of Natural Language Processing (can you tell I’m into NLP yet) and a Machine Learning Practicum course. I completed a really cool Data Science Internship this Summer at BU Spark! and am currently working part-time as a Technical Engineer and a Grader in 2 Data Science courses this Fall at Boston University. I will be graduating this Winter (December 2023) and am on the look-out for Data Analyst, Machine Learning Engineer and Software Developer roles in the US - please let me know if you have any leads!

Coming to the fun stuff, I love reading, dabble in poetry sometimes and make a killer chai (that’s tea btw). My spirit animal is the panda - chaotic, adorable and hungry in all aspects of life.

Hope this website piqued your interest somehow! I had loads of fun creating it :)
